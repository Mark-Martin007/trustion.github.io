FAQ

How does remote support work? ▶
We connect to your computer securely (with your permission) using a remote tool. You watch everything, and can stop at any time. No physical drop-off needed.
Is it safe to give remote access?
▶
Yes, when you control the session and close it when done. Only use companies you trust. We never ask for passwords or banking credentials and only act with your consent.
Can you recover stolen money?
▶
We can improve your chances by guiding evidence collection and reporting/disputes. Outcomes depend on banks/platforms. We never charge upfront “recovery fees.”
What devices do you support?
▶
Windows PCs, Mac computers, and (for security consults) iOS/Android, routers, and small business setups.
How fast can I get help?
▶
Usually same-day—call us to confirm availability! We are fully remote across the USA and Canada.
